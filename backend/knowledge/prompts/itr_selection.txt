ITR-1 (SAHAJ):

ITR-3:

- All income sources allowed
- Partner in a firm
- Regular books of accounts required

- For presumptive taxation (Section 44AD/44ADA/44AE)
- Business turnover up to ₹2 crore (44AD)
- Professional income up to ₹50 lakh (44ADA)
- Goods transport business (44AE)
- Total income up to ₹50 lakh
- Simplified filing, no detailed books needed
